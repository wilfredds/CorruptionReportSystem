Did You Know?

Just 30 minutes of cycling 3x per week reduces your risk of heart disease by up to 50%. Your bike is literally a medicine machine.

Cycling releases endorphins, dopamine, and serotonin — the same chemicals triggered by antidepressant medication. Feeling sad? Go ride.

The Philippines has over 2 million registered cyclists as of 2023. The pandemic cycling boom of 2020 permanently changed Filipino commuting habits.

The average Filipino cyclist who rides 3x per week gains 2–3 extra healthy years of life expectancy, according to global cycling health studies.

Cycling to work saves the average Filipino ₱3,000–₱8,000 per month in commuting costs. That's ₱36,000–₱96,000 per year — enough for a better bike!

Regular cyclists sleep an average of 1 hour more per night than non-cyclists. The physical exertion regulates sleep hormones naturally.

Tagalog cycling term: "Ang mahirap na ruta ay nagbibigay ng pinakamalakas na kasiyahan." The hardest route gives the greatest satisfaction. 🚴‍♂️
